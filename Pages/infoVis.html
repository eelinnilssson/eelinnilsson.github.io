    Purpose

    As a school project we got to come up with a tool that a school could use to learn 13-15 year olds about poverty in the world. As this can be a heavy subject, our group wanted to make an interactive tool where the students would have the possibility to play around with to learn, to make it more interesting. As this was a quite short project, we choose to only make a proof of concept of two aspcets of the tool; Explore the world, and Explore over time. This project was part of the course Information Vizualisation.

    Result

    As the target group for this application is students between 13 and 15 years old, we have made some adjustments to make it easier for this group to take in and understand the data. One way to make it more appealing for the target audience was to add a lot of colors and animations to make it less overwhelming. Anthoher adjustment that has been made is that we present a lot of the information in multiple ways. This has been implemented since everybody learns in different ways, and by giving the opportunity to receive the data in multiple ways, facilitate the userâ€™s learning path.

    This application consists of two different visualizations but they are all connected with the help of this landing page. Since we see this as one application which the three visualizations are a subpart of, some of the functions are common for multiple of the visualizations, for example the zoom and hover function. From this landing page, the user can easily navigate to the two visualizations: Explore countries, and Explore through history.
