TRASH TALK

TRASH TALK is a game where kids get to sort different recycling objects. The kids will place out trash objects in the bins and lock in their answer when they are done. The trash will be scanned by the bins and if it is sorted correctly the trash will fall down and be recycled. Otherwise, the bin turns red and the kids have to try again.

Canvas Group

The goal with Canvas Group is to simplify group projects in school. This app collects all relevant forums that a group needs. The app lets the students to chat with each other, create and edit documents together, and have conference calls.
